Canadian Citizenship

Canada is the best place to migrate to get settled or begin a new life. If you aspire to become a Canadian citizen, apply with Connell today and enjoy the cosmopolitan lifestyle with leading healthcare and education.

Steps to become a Canadian citizen

  • Apply for citizenship
  • Qulaify for the citizenship test and interview
  • Proceed for Visa Application

To be a part of the Citizenship program, check your eligibility, prepare for the test and apply with Connell to experience the joy of a new beginning.

Other Service & Information

In addition to the Permanent residency program, Canada also offers several other services in the citizenship program for the applicants to choose from. These include:

  • Proof of citizenship
  • Find out if you’re already a citizen
  • Give up citizenship
  • Resume citizenship
  • Changes to citizenship rules and requirements

Documents Required/ Other Requirements

The Canadian citizenship program demands other requirements with the official documents listed:

  • Having a net worth of at least CAD$600000
  • Demonstrate business and management experience
  • Eligibility for legal immigration status in Canada
  • Passport & travel history
  • Education and business credentials
