Canada Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) Details

The Provincial Nominee Program targets skilled professionals at the global level. The Canadian provinces requiring specific talents choose the applicants through the Provincial Nominee Program. Many provinces have successfully recruited applicants under this program. Some of the best Canadian provinces include:

  • Prince Edward Island
  • Ontario
  • Saskatchewan
  • Manitoba
  • Nova Scotia
  • Alberta

Skilled professionals with experience in the fields of technology, finance, education, marketing, or healthcare are best suited for the Provincial Nominee Program.

Applicants with the right skills and willingness to contribute to the Canadian economy can apply under the PNP option to become permanent residents.

Options in the Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)

PNP facilitates two options for applying under the program.

  • Paper based process
  • Express Entry Program

Paper based process:

The candidates applying through the paper-based program should apply under the non- Express entry stream. 11 of the 13 provinces and territories in Canada offer the PNP, through which the skilled applicants meeting the eligibility criteria of the respective province can apply. If the applicants qualify through the in-demand occupation list, a nomination from the relative province will be sent to you. You can then apply for a Canada PR visa.

The candidates are required to submit a paper application for the PR visa. The Processing time is much longer compared to the Express Entry Program

Express Entry System:

There are two ways to apply through the Express Entry Stream:

Apply for a nomination at any eligible province or territory under the Express Entry Program or by contacting the province.

If the applied or territory is interested in the application and nominates you, create an Express entry Profile or update if already exists.

The second option is to create an Express Entry profile and apply for the interested province or territory. If the province sends a ‘notice of interest’, contact the province and apply through the Express Entry System for further processing.

The candidates must accomplish the eligibility requirements to get a positive response to their application.

Documents Required

The documents for the PNP program required by the applicants include:

  • Passport and travel history
  • Educational credentials
  • Professional credentials
  • Recent medical report
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Other supporting documents
