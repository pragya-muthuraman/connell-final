Settle with your Family in Canada

Become a Canadian citizen and settle with your family. Canada offers permanent residencies to eligible dependents or family members.

Candidates already settled in Canada and wanting to move their dependants with them can apply under the program.

The program initiates the reuniting of the family members with citizenship opportunities.

To facilitate families to live together, the Government of Canada allows eligible residents above the age of 18 to sponsor dependent spouses, children, parents, partners, and grandparents to live with them in Canada

Canada Dependent Visa Details

Under the Canada Dependent Visa, the candidates can bring their dependents to Canada and also offers them work or studying opportunity with the relevant permit. The candidates can sponsor the following relationships through the Canada Dependent Visa:

  • Spouse or a conjugal partner
  • Dependent children under the age of 21
  • A child you adopted outside Canada with Canadian citizenship or a permanent residency
  • A brother, sister, niece, nephew, aunt, uncle, or other close relatives

The sponsored relations can live with the candidate in Canada. In the case of a spouse or conjugal partner, a work permit can be offered.

Eligibility Requirements to Sponsor a Dependent

  • The candidate must be 18 years or above
  • Must be a Canadian citizen or a permanent resident
  • Should not be taking government aid, except in case of a disability
  • Must be in the low-income threshold
  • Should not have a contract-based relationship

Documents Required for Canada Dependent Visa

  • Passport information and travel history
  • Documentation to prove background
  • Marriage certificate and other documentation
  • Proof of relationship
  • Proof of income for fund adequacy
  • Completed application and consulate fees

Eligibility Requirements to Sponsor a Spouse

  • You must be 18 years old.
  • Must meet the spouse/ Partner’s basic financial needs for 3 years.
  • The candidate must live in Canada or return after the spouse/ partner receives a permanent residency.
  • To sponsor your partner, the candidate must be a citizen, a permanent resident, or have a work visa.
  • The marriage must not be contract based and should be a year-long.

Documents Required

Sponsoring a dependent under the Canadian Dependent Visa requires the following documentation:

  • Passport & travel history
  • Background documentation
  • Spouse/partner documentation including marriage certificate
  • Other proof of relationship
  • Proof of income of the sponsor to show adequate finances
  • Completed application & consulate fees
