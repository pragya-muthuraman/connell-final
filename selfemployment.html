Self Employment in Canada

A talented approach for students and professionals with skills of a lifetime.

Self-employed candidates include business people, freelancers, artists, actors, broadcasters, writers, etc. The program offers permanent residency for the students based on the eligible criteria.

Candidates qualifying the eligibility criteria and looking to establish a business or buy a business have high success rates.

The candidates setting up the business must profit the growth of Canada in the slightest way possible.

Being a source of new and steady employment creation as well as a chief contributor to the economy of Canada can apply through this immigration pathway.

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) Details

The self-employment program is a part of the business immigration pathways in Canada. The candidate must be self-employed and must contribute to Canada’s artistic, social or cultural life.

  • The self-employment program is a point-based system with points credited to each category.
  • The category includes age, education, experience, language proficiency, and adaptability.
  • The candidates are required to score a minimum of 35 points in the eligibility criteria.
  • Self-employment is a time-consuming process and takes up to 15-22 months to complete the entire process.

Entrepreneur Immigration

This program consists of three streams.

  • Entrepreneur Immigration Category
  • Entrepreneur Immigration — Regional Pilot
  • Strategic Projects Category

Entrepreneur Immigration Category

This stream is for entrepreneurs who wish to set up a business in the province. The invitation under this system is also points-based. The applicants must have the required funds to invest in a business. They will be eligible for PR under the entrepreneur immigration stream.

Entrepreneur Immigration — Regional Pilot

This is for investors wishing to start a new business in the regional communities across the province.

Strategic Projects Category

This is for investors wishing to start a new business in the regional communities across the province.

Eligibility Requirements for Entrepreneur Immigration Category

  • A personal net worth of at least $600,000 .
  • Create at least one position for a Canadian citizen or permanent citizen in the country
  • Invest a minimum of $200,000 in a new or existing business
  • Obtain a level 4 in the Canadian Language Benchmark in all competencies of listening, speaking, reading, and writing in English or French.
  • Experience in business management.
  • The candidate must hold temporary immigration criteria or be eligible for it.

Eligibility Requirements for Regional Pilot

  • A minimum net worth of $300,000.
  • The candidate must hold temporary immigration criteria or be eligible for it.
  • A minimum of 51 percent in the acquired business.
  • Obtain a level 4 in the Canadian Language Benchmark in all competencies of listening, speaking, reading, and writing in English or French.
  • Create at least one full-time employee for a Canadian Citizen or a Permanent Resident
  • An exploratory visit to the target community.

Eligibility Requirements for Strategic Projects Category

  • Make a $500,000 minimum equity investment.
  • For each foreign essential staff member recommended, create at least three new employments for Canadian citizens or permanent residents
  • Establish a business or buy an existing one and expand.
