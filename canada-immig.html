Moving abroad is an individual decision and varies relatively. A majority of the people choose to either study or work abroad preferring global exposure and working in the international markets or to expand their lifestyle possibilities.

Canada offers a cosmopolitan lifestyle and stabilized income, inviting more people to immerse themselves in the culture.

The eminent urbanity holds a welcome sign to all its immigrants from around the world. Diversity is celebrated and hence the great demand for migrants with skills and talents. Canada offers a platform for excellence to all people willing to move in.

Canada also cherishes permanent residencies, providing the best premises to start a new life.

    Why Canada ?

    Canada proves to be a leading destination for immigrants and welcomes migrants at a global level. Here are a few reasons why:

    • Free education and healthcare facilities
    • An inviting environment for talented immigrants
    • Convenient immigrant process
    • Vibrant cities and a rich culture
    • A safe and sound place to settle in

    It is much easier to migrate to Canada compared to other major English- speaking countries. Canada welcomes migrants from all around the globe to enhance the diversified culture.

    Who can Apply ?

    Though migration is easier in Canada, the process follows a certain eligibility criterion for assessing the applicants. Each immigration program lays its criteria for selection, the most common ones are:

    • Educational background
    • Professional profile
    • IELTS? CELPIP score
    • Proficiency in French (If migrating to Quebec)
    • References & legal documentation
    • Canadian employment documentation
